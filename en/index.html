The artist

Classical piano meets alternative rock

beranger blends classical piano with the energy of alternative rock (Linkin Park, Muse, Panic! At The Disco). From busking to sold-out shows at Kesselhaus and Lido, and performances at Lollapalooza and Festsaal Kreuzberg. He has worked with Michael Ilbert (Muse, Coldplay) and Moses Schneider (AnnenMayKantereit, Beatsteaks), filmed videos with the piano brand Casio and been featured by Shure. Behind millions of organic Spotify streams is a live show that stays with you.
